BMI CALCULATER

BMI, or Body Mass Index, is a numerical value calculated from a person's height and weight. It is used as a screening tool to categorize individuals into different weight groups, helping to assess whether they are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

Although BMI is a widely used method, it does not measure body fat directly and may not always account for differences in muscle mass, bone density, or distribution of fat.
